COLLEGE BUILDING

DSEU's Dwarka campus is located in Sector-9, Dwarka. It is dedicated to imparting quality vocational higher education and training. Besides its world-class infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, the centre is devoted to assisting students in choosing a vocation for life.

CLASSROOM

XYZ is the foundation stone for a child’s future.

The College prepares the students not to face the academic challenges only but to develop physical, social and psychological skill too. we have around 201 class rooms, which are spacious,airy,equipped with smart class, comfortable seating, modular furniture, display board. These ensures a close interaction with each student.

COMPUTER LABS

There are Six computer labs for junior and senior students as well. They are equipped with 100 computers High-Tech- multimedia computer labs are indeed a boon for BVMIANS.

SMART CLASS

At XYZ College, New Delhi, we have the Smart Class concept also in practice. What might otherwise seem dull or monotonous with just text-book studies, becomes bright, and captivating in the flash and animation movies, which re-affirm the concepts already taught in class. This makes the process of learning fun and also ensures the lasting impact on the minds of today’s generation. Over all it makes learning fun and improve academic performance.

COLLEGE LIBRARY

Reading adds newer dimensions to the body of knowledge. XYZ, believe in inculcating a reading culture that enhances and compliments the education system. Books that capture the imagination of students reconfirm their facts and open new doors of intellect are carefully chosen and made available to the students. Books on varied subject are available here.

Reference Books and Magazines keep the students abreast with all that’s happening around. Students are encouraged to give their suggestion about the kind of books they would like in the library, and our librarians do their bit to carry their suggestions further.
